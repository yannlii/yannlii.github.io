Finding My Cello

The name was inspired by the cellist Yo-Yo Ma, who regards cello as his life calling. I’ve always admired his passion for music and kept reminding myself that it’s never too late to find my own cello, and the time has now come.

If I ever get the chance to do more with these photos, I’ll set up an exhibition on the ruins - all abandoned buildings are deafeningly silent when visitors pass by, with only whispers of stories behind images. That’s exactly what I felt when I took pictures and why I called them overheard.
