overheard

fragments and what remains

2023

我在毕业论文的致谢中写过：“在每一个不得不原路返回的十字路口，马友友（Yo-Yo Ma）对大提琴始终如一的热情，以及淡豹关于成为一个不被打断的人的想象，一直是我精神世界里的无形支柱。” 马友友把大提琴视为一生的使命，我也时常用 find your own cello 提醒自己去寻找那把属于自己的大提琴。 于是在 Cello Suite No.1 的单曲循环里，赶在毕业之前完成了这个网站。

如果继续拍下去，我还想策划一场展览：把照片挂在某个街道或公园的树上，或者挂在废弃的建筑空地里，散步的人经过时，四周安静只剩下照片诉说背后故事的声音——这是 overheard 的由来。

In the acknowledgements of my graduation thesis, I once wrote: “At every crossroads where I had to turn back, Yo-Yo Ma’s unwavering devotion to the cello, and Dan Bao’s pursuit of being a person undisturbed, have been invisible anchors in my inner world.” With Cello Suite No.1 on repeat, I finished this website just before graduation. Yo-Yo Ma treats the cello as his life’s calling. I, too, kept reminding myself with find your own cello—to seek the thing that truly belongs to me.

2026

恍恍惚惚又三年，网站也生了灰。 这里的大多数照片来自那台被我称作「口袋朋友」的 Olympus Mju II，直到 2023 年底它被我丢在了那不勒斯的海边。

爷爷去世之后，我几乎不再拍胶片。每次想整理已经洗好的底片，都不知道会在哪一卷里突然翻到和他一起逛公园的照片，于是一再搁置。

在津巴布韦的新年夜，我独自坐在新家的阳台上赏月。忽然想起苏轼写过的那句：

“料得年年肠断处，明月夜，短松冈。”

Three years passed in a haze, and the website had gathered dust. Most of these photos were taken with my “pocket friend,” an Olympus Mju II, which I left on the Naples shore at the end of 2023. After my grandfather passed, I barely touched film. Every time I thought of going through developed rolls, I hesitated—never knowing which one might suddenly reveal a park walk with him—so I put them aside again and again. On New Year’s Eve in Zimbabwe, I sat alone on the balcony of my new home, gazing at the moon. A line from Su Shi came to mind: “Grief returns year after year, under the bright moon, on Short Pine Hill.”
